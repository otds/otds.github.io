Opportunities in China – How We Can Help You

We know the decision to expand to offshore markets can be overwhelming and is compounded by language and complex cultural differences, unfamiliar regulations and sheer scale. We exist to prepare and ease the passage of your business with China's markets .

Expansion into China may mean many different things for small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs); eg it may mean

  • Importing or exporting components, finished goods or services
  • Establishing manufacturing relationships
  • Developing greenfield operations or joint ventures

Each type of relationship will have a different set of complex requirements to be managed in isolation and in the context of the business as a whole and these pressure limited internal resources.

HDWS offers relevant cross-cultural qualifications and background to assist SME owners:

  • Confirm that the business is ready to explore offshore markets
  • Confirm that China offers the most viable conditions for their offshore expansion
  • Identify the scope of requirements to make it ready where it is not ready
  • Gauge if the potential risks attached to the expansion are manageable in relation to the existing business model and as a stand-alone business unit
  • Decide criteria in qualifying Chinese counterparts
  • Determine the desired outcomes through the expansion
  • Develop long term business relationship with Chinese counterparts
  • Confirm the MIS's comprehensive facility to monitor and evaluate the expansion vsv the business whole

HDWS works closely with your organisation to ensure each phase is documented for reference and ongoing evaluation purposes and includes:

  • Evaluation Report – Off-shore Expansion Ready?
  • Expansion/Strategic Plan
  • Implementation Plan
  • Periodic Reviews
  • Periodical Updates
