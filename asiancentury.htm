Australia in the Asian Century

In November 2012, the Australian Prime Minister Julia Gillard released a White Paper titled Australia in the Asian Century, which outlines the " roadmap for the whole of Australia -governments, business, unions, and the broader community ... to secure Australia as a more prosperous and resilient nation that is fully part of our region and open to the world."

The White Paper outlines 5 key areas in which Australia needs to excel to succeed in this Asian Century. HDWS is concerned in particular with the 3 rd and 5 th areas where…

… Australian firms and institutions are called on to "develop collaborative relationships with others in the region" and to be "highly innovative (and) competitive" and
… They will "need new business models and new mindsets to operate and connect with Asian markets" … "to seize the opportunities created in our region."

An Asian Century Fact Sheet states that "Organisations that are successful in the Asian century will have people who have specific knowledge of the products and markets of Asia, along with the cultural and language capabilities needed to be active in the region. Businesses are taking action, and some are already well advanced, but more needs to be done."

HDWS possesses the literacy and business capabilities required to assist Australian businesses with their expansion into, in particular, China's market. We know that many Australian businesses are commercially ready now or near-ready to expand offshore and that the time needed to build internal Asia literacy and capacity is against them.

HDWS exists to bridge the cultural and commercial gaps that will make your business's offshore expansion plans to China an exciting rather than daunting discovery, whether you are ready now, preparing to enter or considering entry in the foreseeable future.
