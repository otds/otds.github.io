About Us

HDWS Consulting specialises in advising Australian and Asian invested small to medium sized businesses (SMEs ) in Australia particularly in the areas of project analysis & evaluation, financial performance, business process management & efficiencies and establishing and implementing business strategy. Founder Ms. Emma Hou has worked for the past 20 years in both Australia and China in business sectors ranging from large public financial institutions to private import and export companies.

Emma realised her goal to transition from employee to independent contractor and consultant shortly after her career began in Australia in 2001. Her exposure to large public financial institutions includes having been a Project Analyst at NAB, Property Analyst at Colonial First State Property Management and Management Accountant with Australian National Line and Dun & Bradstreet.  She has also worked with SMEs in the mining, education and fashion industries.

Before moving from China to Australia in 1999, Emma was a departmental Finance Manager with Tianjin Stationery & Sporting Goods Import & Export . The agency represents more than 1,000 manufacturers across 20 provinces and municipalities and manages trade relations with more than 10,000 firms across more than 120 countries.   Emma was charged with simultaneously managing a ground-breaking Export Agency Pilot Project for which she also undertook new business development responsibilities.  Further, she initiated and was instrumental in introducing mechanisms to monitor and promote accountability within the business. 

Emma gained private enterprise experience in China as Senior Financial Controller for family-owned businesses in freight forwarding and commercial property (Origence Transport (Tianjin) Pty. Ltd. & Origence Property (Tianjin) Pty. Ltd.) particularly in China's fourth largest city, Tianjin. She was inspired to move to Australia in particular for its standards in higher education where she attained two masters degrees and certifications as a CPA, MPA, MFin, GDip FP and CSACert.

Under Emma Hou's stewardship, HDWS's guiding philosophy comes from an old Chinese proverb: When confronted by mountains, one finds a way through. When blocked by a river, one finds a way to bridge the other side .  Emma's cross cultural business knowledge with finance & accounting acumen underscores her capacity to support Australian and Chinese businesses wanting to, or ready to explore opportunities in one another's markets.
